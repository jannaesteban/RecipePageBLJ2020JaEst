LET'S BAKE

1. Step

Beat the soft butter or margarine with the sugar, salt and egg yolks to a light cream.

2.Step

Mix flour with baking powder and gradually stir briefly and vigorously.

3.Step

Mix flour with baking powder gradually stir briefly and vigorously.

4. Step

.Add the ground nuts and the chopped chocolate

5. Step

Add milk. Finally, lift the egg white.

6. Step

Mix them all together

7. Step

Wreath shape and fill the dough

8. step

Bake in a preheated oven at 175°C circulating air on the middle rail for about 50 -70 minutes.
